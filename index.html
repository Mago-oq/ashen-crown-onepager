What is the game?

Ashen Crown is a dark fantasy visual novel and kingdom simulator where you play as a young demon ruler trying to save a broken empire. You hear petitions, negotiate with ministers, manage resources, command armies and decide whether mercy, fear or pragmatism will hold the realm together.

Genre Visual Novel + Kingdom Sim
Perspective Young Demon Ruler
Core Conflict Civil War + Collapse
Tone Dark Fantasy + Political Drama
